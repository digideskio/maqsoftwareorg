Uplift and empower communities through technology focused philanthropy

We believe advances in AI will enable improved teaching that can be individualized based on the learning pace and ability of the students. MAQ Software is investing in the latest technical advances to transform primary and secondary education in India for low income families.

The challenge

Over 100 million school children in India are unable to study at their school level because of multiple issues such as, non availability of good teachers, basic infrastructure not being available. Also, they are unable to afford modern equipments and gadgets that use the latest technology for education.

100 Million

Children unable to study at Grade Level

Our area of focus

As a technology solution provider, MAQ Software is in a unique position to survey advances in AI, gaming, virtual reality, and cell phones to provide India with technology-driven education solutions. For the next 25 years, MAQ Software will invest in technology advances to improve primary and secondary education in India. To start, MAQ Software will provide three-year grants to leading NGOs to sponsor research at IITs, NITs, IIITs and IIMs, and adopt solutions from them.

Collaborate with Pratham to build quality software

Pratham is one of the largest non-governmental organisations in India. It was co-founded by Madhav Chavan and Farida Lambay. It works towards the provision of quality education to the underprivileged children in India.
